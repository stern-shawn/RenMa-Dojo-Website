Beginning Kendo

What You Need to Know to Get Started

What You'll Learn

Kendo is a rigorous martial art, and regular practice will help you to develop not only physically, but mentally as well. The lessons you learn as a beginner will apply all throughout your kendo career, and over time you will find that they apply to your normal life, not just in the dojo. As a beginner at Ren Ma Dojo, you will be introduced to the following kendo basics and encouraged to develop to the best of your ability as you decide if kendo is right for you:

  • Traditional etiquette
  • Terminology
  • Basic footwork
  • Fundamentals of holding and swinging the sword
  • The primary striking targets of kendo
  • The Kendo Kata (forms)

In addition to this kendo-specific knowledge, you will also develop:

  • Discipline
  • Perseverance
  • Respect for others and yourself
  • Physical stamina, strength, and flexibility
